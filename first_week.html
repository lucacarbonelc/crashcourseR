1 Welcome to the R Crash Course!

R is an open source programming language, extremely versatile and increasingly used in academia and industry for data analysis. Together with RMarkdown, RStudio can be employed to build personal websites or pages (like this one!).


R is good for several reasons:

• It’s open source (you can always check what happens under the hood);
• New methods are continuously implemented;
• Great R community (developers and users) online, where to always find (or ask for) help. For example, StackOverFlow;
• Optimal for open science: clear syntax that makes easy to replicate results;
• It’s free.
